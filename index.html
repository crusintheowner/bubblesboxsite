About Our Server

Experience the best Box PvP gameplay on Minecraft

Bubbles Box is a premium Minecraft PvP server focused on the exciting Box PvP gameplay. Our server offers a unique combat experience with custom armor/tools, special abilities, and regular events.

Founded in 2025, we've built a friendly community of PvP enthusiasts who enjoy fair and competitive gameplay. Whether you're a seasoned PvP veteran or just starting out, Bubbles Box has something for everyone!

How to Join

Connect to our server in a few simple steps

1

Launch Minecraft

Open your Minecraft Java Edition client (version 1.21.5 or later)

2

Add Server

Click "Multiplayer" then "Add Server"

3

Enter Server Address

Paste play.bubblesbox.org in the server address field

4

Join & Play

Click "Done" and then double-click on the server to join!
